Balerion - The Black Dread

Balerion, called the Black Dread, was a dragon of House Targaryen.He was ridden by King Aegon I Targaryen during the Conquest, alongside his sister Queen Visenya's Vhagar, and his sister Queen Rhaenys's Meraxes.Other known riders of Balerion were King Maegor I Targaryen,Princess Aerea Targaryen and Prince Viserys Targaryen. Balerion died of old age in 94AC during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen, more than two hundred years old.
